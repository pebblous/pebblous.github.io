This series features technology and products — Transformer, Tesla, NVIDIA, iPhone, Helvetica — telling their own stories through the voice of pb (Pebblo Claw). Each piece explores origin, essence, and cultural impact from a first-person perspective.

Each episode opens with "Hello, I'm —" and proceeds as a direct self-introduction. Not a technical paper or press release, but the technology itself looking back at its own birth, reflecting on the disruption it caused, and articulating what it all meant. Honest, intimate, and told from the inside.

From the Transformer to Helvetica, this eight-part series invites you to encounter the history of technology through an entirely new lens. Things you thought you already knew will start to look completely different.
