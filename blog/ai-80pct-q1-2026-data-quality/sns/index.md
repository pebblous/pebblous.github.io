## LinkedIn (KO)

2026년 1분기, 전 세계 벤처 자금 5달러 중 4달러가 AI 한 분야로 갔다.

Crunchbase 집계로 글로벌 투자 3000억 달러 가운데 80%가 AI 기업으로 향했다. 1년 전 같은 비중이 55%였던 것에 비하면 급등한 사상 최대 분기다. 그중에서도 OpenAI·Anthropic·xAI·Waymo 단 네 회사가 전체 투자의 65%를 가져갔다.

그런데 이 돈은 대부분 모델과 컴퓨트로 갔다. 정작 그 모델을 신뢰할 수 있게 만드는 일, 검증된 독점 데이터와 그 품질을 보증하는 역량에는 거의 닿지 않았다.

돈으로 더 큰 모델은 살 수 있어도, 우리 조직의 데이터가 AI-Ready인지는 수표로 해결되지 않는다. 사들일 수 없는 역량은 자본이 모델로 쏠릴수록 오히려 더 희소해진다.

이 자본 집중이 끝났을 때 가치가 남는 자리는, 돈이 채워 주지 못한 바로 그 빈자리다.

## LinkedIn (EN)

In Q1 2026, four of every five venture dollars in the world went to a single field: AI.

Of roughly $300B in global venture funding, 80% flowed to AI companies, up sharply from 55% a year earlier — a record quarter. Within that, just four firms (OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Waymo) absorbed 65% of all global investment.

But most of that money went to models and compute. The work that makes those models trustworthy — securing verified proprietary data and vouching for its quality — barely saw any of it.

Capital can buy a bigger model and more GPUs. It cannot tell you whether your own data is AI-Ready. And a capability you cannot buy only grows scarcer as capital piles into models.

When this concentration ends, value will remain in exactly the gap money left empty.

## Facebook (EN)

"Four dollars out of five."

That is what this quarter's global venture funding comes down to, if you shrink it to a single line. And every one of those four dollars went to AI.

The number is striking. Yet what stayed with me longer was not the number, but the place the money never reached.

Capital bought bigger models and more compute. But what sets the ceiling on a model is the quality and the rights of the data it feeds on. You can buy GPUs with a check. You cannot buy your way to knowing whether your own data is AI-Ready.

So one question lingers.

"When this concentration of capital ends, where will the value remain?"

Are you standing on a model someone can copy in six months, or on data — and the quality behind it — that no one can easily replicate? That is close to why Pebblous has kept talking about AI-Ready Data for so long.

The gap money left empty is the one I keep returning to these days.
