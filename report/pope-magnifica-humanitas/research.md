## 2. 단락별 핵심 인용 (verbatim, 단락 번호 확정)

> ⚠️ 우리 보고서 보강 시 그대로 인용 가능. EWTN의 "15 quotes" + America/Pillar/Angelus 단락 번호 교차 검증.

### §1 — 개막 명제 (Babel vs Jerusalem)

> "Humanity, created by God in all its grandeur, is today facing a pivotal choice: **either to construct a new Tower of Babel or to build the city in which God and humanity dwell together.**"

### §3 — Rerum Novarum 135주년 직접 명시

> "In this spirit, Pope Leo XIII published his Encyclical *Rerum Novarum* in 1891, **the 135th anniversary of which we celebrate with deep gratitude this year**."

### §4 — 새로운 인간 권력

> "Never has humanity had such power over itself."

### §7 — 바벨 신화 재해석

> "Babel thus reveals the limits of any effort that, however grandiose, arises from self-affirmation, sacrifices human dignity for efficiency and aspires to reach heaven without God's blessing."

### §9 — **기술의 비중립성 (가장 자주 인용됨)**

> "**Technology is never neutral**, because it takes on the characteristics of those who devise, finance, regulate and use it... the primary choice is not between a 'yes' or 'no' to technology, but rather between **constructing Babel or rebuilding Jerusalem**."

### §10 — Babel syndrome

> "We must, then, avoid the '**Babel syndrome**,' namely **the idolatry of profit that sacrifices the weak**, a uniformity that neutralizes differences, and the pretense that a single language — even a digital one — can translate everything, including the mystery of the person, into data and performance."

### §15 — "어떤 기계도 대체할 수 없는"

> "In the era of artificial intelligence, when human dignity is threatened... we must lovingly safeguard the grandeur of humanity bestowed upon us and revealed in its fullness in Christ, **the splendor of which no machine can ever replace**."

### §39 — 사유재산의 도덕적 종속성

> "The positive potential of the market and private initiative [makes sense] only if they remain subordinate to the moral law."

### §66 — **재산권의 비절대성 (America 캐피탈리즘 비판 핵심)**

> "The Christian tradition has never recognized **the right to private property as absolute or inviolable**."

### §76 — 데이터에 대한 연대 책임

> "Solidarity demands that decisions regarding data, algorithms... take into account... impact on all peoples and future generations."

### §99 — AI는 인간 지능 아님

> "AI systems do not undergo experiences, do not possess a body, do not feel joy or pain."

### §103 — 알고리즘이 가리는 책임

> "The exclusion of the vulnerable becomes cloaked in a veneer of neutrality."

### §104 — 모든 기술은 가치 내장

> "Every technical tool embodies choices and priorities through what it measures, ignores."

### §108 — **데이터=공공재 (Aleteia 핵심 6 중 4)**

> "Data should not be treated as something to be sold off or entrusted to a select few."  
> "Among the goods that are universally intended for everyone, we must also include **new forms of property, such as patents, algorithms, digital platforms, technological infrastructure and data**."

### §109 — **느림(slowing down)의 정치적 책임**

> "[We need] a more active political involvement capable of **slowing things down when everything is accelerating**."

### §110 — **"disarm" 단어의 의도 (Angelus: "교황의 마음에 가까운 표현")**

> "[We must disarm AI] from the mentality of armed competition... **freeing technology from monopolistic control** and opening it to discussion and debate, therefore making it human-friendly."

### §111 — 모든 설계는 인간관의 반영

> "Every design choice reflects a vision of humanity."

### §125 — **AI 무장해제 완전 인용 (가장 많이 인용된 한 문장)**

> "In a similar sense, **artificial intelligence now demands to be 'disarmed,' freed from logics that turn it into an instrument of domination, exclusion and death.**"

### §128 — 인간 vs 알고리즘의 오류관

> "For an algorithm, an error is a flaw; for a person, an error can be a catalyst for profound change."

### §141 — 청소년 디바이스 위험

> "[Early mobile device use] can exacerbate vulnerabilities, foster addiction, and expose [young people] to isolation, bullying."

### §173 — **숨겨진 노동자 사슬**

> "[Behind AI systems lies] a long chain of mediation [involving millions in invisible labor]."

### §176 — **노예제에 대한 교회의 역사적 늦장 사과**

> "For this, in the name of the Church, I sincerely ask for pardon."

### §197-198 — **자율무기, 정의로운 전쟁론의 종언**

> "It is not permissible to entrust lethal or otherwise irreversible decisions to artificial systems."  
> "**There exists no algorithm that can make war morally acceptable.**" (§198)  
> The 'just war' theory which has all too often been used to justify any kind of war, is now outdated."

### §211, 213 — 사랑의 문명, 작은 충실의 누적

> "The civilization of love arises from the sum total of **small and steadfast acts of fidelity**."

### §233 — 마음과 양심은 계산되지 않는다

> "No computational system can create a heart that gives itself, or a conscience that discerns good from evil."

### §237 — 진실에 충실한 마음

> "Cultivate hearts that love the truth, prefer what is right despite the most appealing content."

## 6. 톤·프레임 결정 권고 (재기획 input)

### 옵션 A — **순수 SEO 강화** (톤·구조 그대로)
- 현 제목 "135년 만의 질문 — 자율인가, 연대인가" 유지
- 메타 description/keywords에 무장해제·디지털 노예제·바벨탑 모두 박기
- 본문에 §9, §110, §125, §173, §198 직접 인용 4~5개 삽입
- 1시간 작업

### 옵션 B — **하이브리드** (시그니처 톤 + 정책 프레임 보강)
- 본문 H1·시그니처 제목 유지
- `<title>` 태그만 SEO 친화 ("교황 레오 14세 '무장해제' 회칙 Magnifica Humanitas — 245항 5장의 인간 존엄 선언")
- 5섹션 → 7섹션 확장: "회칙이 정의한 무장해제(§110/§125)" + "디지털 노예제와 새로운 노동 사슬(§173)" 신설
- 2~3시간 작업, KO+EN 동시

### 옵션 C — **전면 재구성** (외신 합의 프레임 5종 모두 챕터화)
- 현 글을 v1로 보존, v2를 별도로
- 5장 챕터에 1:1 대응하는 7개 섹션
- 4~5시간, 사실상 새 글

### Pebblous 시그니처 보존 vs SEO 트래픽 — 트레이드오프

**JH 보이스 정책**: "차별화 톤 유지하되 핵심 키워드는 본문에 자연스럽게 박는다" (docs/title-strategy.md)

→ **권고: 옵션 B**. 우리의 "자율 vs 연대" 각도는 외신에 없는 독자적 통찰이므로 살리고, 회칙이 직접 쓴 §110("disarm"), §125("disarmed from logics of domination"), §198(no algorithm) 인용을 두 새 섹션으로 추가하여 시그니처 + SEO 모두 잡는다.
