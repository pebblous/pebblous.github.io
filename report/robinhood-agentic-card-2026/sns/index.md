## LinkedIn (EN)

The first consumer product that lets AI spend on your behalf went live on May 27.

Robinhood launched an agentic credit card for its 800,000-plus Gold members. Set a spending limit; the AI agent executes purchases within it. Mastercard and Visa rolled out their own agentic token infrastructure in the same year, making the payment ecosystem's shift to agent-ready rails official.

Tokenization handled the security layer. Visa reports a 40% reduction in fraud on tokenized transactions.

But tokenization only hides the card number. It does not verify that the AI is making the right purchase decision. Academic benchmarks place financial AI agents at a 39.3% pass rate. Security is solved; data trust is the open problem.

The next frontier in autonomous payments is not model speed — it is the accuracy of the data agents rely on.
