## LinkedIn (EN)

The data broker market is worth $313B. Data marketplace platforms? Just $1.8B. That is a 170x gap.

Web3 is attempting to close it. With 300+ DataDAOs, 41.8 million DePIN devices, and Helium reaching 2 million daily active users, the infrastructure for individuals to own and trade their data directly is no longer theoretical.

But one critical layer is missing. Payment protocols like x402 and AP2 are operational. What does not exist yet is a "quality oracle" -- a trust layer that lets AI agents verify the data they are buying in real time. If Chainlink is the price feed oracle for DeFi, the data quality oracle is the missing piece for the agent economy.

Pebblous is building that layer through DataClinic's simulation-based quality diagnostics and ISO/IEC 5259 certification infrastructure.
