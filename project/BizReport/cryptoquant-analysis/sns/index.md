## LinkedIn (EN)

$9.44 million in total funding. 10,000+ monthly media citations. 150+ institutional clients. These are CryptoQuant's numbers.

Founded in 2018 by Ki Young Ju, a POSTECH alumnus, CryptoQuant has built what many call "the Bloomberg of crypto" -- an on-chain data analytics platform that became the first and only on-chain data provider on CME Group's DataMine marketplace in July 2022.

The core moat is not capital. It is data. CryptoQuant maintains proprietary labeling databases that identify hot and cold wallets across hundreds of exchanges. This labeling accuracy is what enabled early warnings of the Terra-Luna collapse and FTX insolvency -- days before the market realized what was happening.

For perspective: Chainalysis raised $537M. CryptoQuant built comparable brand recognition with 1/57th of that capital. That capital efficiency alone makes this a case worth studying.

Pebblous is building a data quality middleware layer for on-chain analytics -- labeling accuracy verification, cross-chain consistency checks, and regulatory audit trails that platforms like CryptoQuant do not currently provide.
