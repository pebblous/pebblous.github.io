## LinkedIn (EN)

USDC market cap at $78.8B, revenue at $2.7B. Circle is the only stablecoin issuer that has turned regulatory compliance from a cost center into a competitive moat.

While most crypto companies avoid regulation, Circle moved in the opposite direction. It obtained Europe's first e-money license under MiCA, proactively prepared for the GENIUS Act, and completed its voluntary audit framework through its June 2025 NYSE listing (CRCL). The revenue model -- 85-90% from US Treasury yields on USDC reserves -- is elegantly simple, though a structural dependency of distributing 51% of revenue to Coinbase remains.

What makes this story forward-looking is Circle's next move: AI agent-to-agent automatic payment infrastructure through the x402 protocol. AI agents call APIs and auto-settle in USDC via HTTP 402 responses. This shifts stablecoins from "store of value" to "machine-to-machine payment protocol."
