## 1. Introduction: The New Competitive Landscape of the AI Wars

"The AI industry is entering an era of fierce competition beyond technological possibility -- the phase of **'The Great Competition Wars.'**"

As PitchBook's '2026 Artificial Intelligence Outlook' report points out, in this new competitive landscape, only companies that go beyond mere technological capability to establish clear market positioning and sustainable competitive advantages -- that is, **technological 'Moats'** -- will survive and thrive.

This analysis report evaluates the business outlook of **Pebblous Inc.** and its flagship platform **AADS (Agentic AI Data Scientist)** through the strategic framework of market trends, AI-era winner conditions, and competitive dynamics as presented by PitchBook.

## 2. Key Market Trends and Opportunity Analysis

Pebblous is strategically responding to the globally accelerating **Sovereign AI trend**. Through a strategic partnership with the Korea Institute of Science and Technology Information (KISTI), Pebblous has adopted the domestic foundation model **KONI LLM/VLM** as the core brain of AADS.

## 3. PitchBook's 'AI Winner' Conditions

Simply entering a promising market cannot guarantee long-term success. True winners must build competitive advantages that are difficult to replicate -- that is, **'Moats.'**

#### 1. Network Effects

A virtuous cycle where more users lead to more data accumulation, improved algorithms, and further user acquisition

#### 2. Unique Data Moats

Possessing unique and valuable data assets that competitors cannot easily replicate or purchase

#### 3. Superior Design and Usability

Interfaces and UX that enable intuitive understanding and utilization of complex AI technologies

#### 4. Large Niche Market Dominance

Targeting large niche markets with high entry barriers and specialized knowledge requirements instead of fiercely competitive general markets

#### 5. Becoming a System of Record

Evolving from a one-time tool into an essential system that manages and records an organization's core data

#### 6. Deep Domain Expertise

In-depth knowledge and data in specific industries and domains that general-purpose models cannot replicate

#### 7. Regulatory Compliance

The ability to leverage strengthening AI regulations as business opportunities rather than risks

#### 8. Creative Distribution Strategy

Customer acquisition approaches where products spread organically or naturally integrate into existing workflows
