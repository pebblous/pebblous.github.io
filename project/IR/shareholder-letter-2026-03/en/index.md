## Our Core Competency: Problem-Solving Through 'Data Farming'

Pebblous is a **'synthetic data platform'** company that enables AI to learn beyond the limitations of reality.

Data on 'rare but critical' hazardous situations that autonomous vehicles and smart factory robots may face is nearly impossible to collect in the real world. Pebblous solves this through physics-based simulation technology. Like cultivating optimal crops in a smart farm, we precisely 'farm' the data that AI needs most in digital environments.

Our core solution, **DataClinic**, is supplied to the Ministry of National Defense and Army Test & Evaluation Command as a Public Procurement Service innovative product, and leading partners including LG U+ and Seoul National University Hospital have chosen Pebblous for AI data quality management.

Furthermore, Pebblous is developing the next-generation platform [Data Greenhouse](/project/DataClinic/data-greenhouse/), where AI agents autonomously perform data diagnosis, generation, and optimization, along with [PebbloSim](/project/PebbloSim/pebblosim-design-strategy/), which generates high-quality synthetic data free of 'physical hallucination' by perfectly replicating the laws of physics. The [Physical AI](/project/PhysicalAI/en/) market these technologies serve is directly linked to Korea's core industries — manufacturing, mobility, and defense — and is the domain where Pebblous can provide data infrastructure at the forefront.

This year, Pebblous is preparing for a quantum leap centered on three strategic pillars.

By forming consortiums with experts across manufacturing, defense, and mobility, we will secure large-scale projects and build a distinctive ecosystem at the intersection of the physical world and AI.
