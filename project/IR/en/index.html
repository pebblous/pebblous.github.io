Since its founding in November 2021, Pebblous Inc. has been building advanced data management technology grounded in the conviction that data quality determines AI performance. After securing seed funding shortly after incorporation, the company completed its Pre-A round in October 2022, reaching KRW 3.1 billion (~USD 2.2M) in cumulative investment and validating market expectations. Over the past years, Pebblous has established its business foundation through DataClinic, PebbloScope, and innovative synthetic data solutions — collaborating with global enterprises including Hyundai Motor, LG Electronics, and LG U+. As of 2026, Pebblous stands at a new inflection point for greater growth, backed by proven technology.

In 2025, Pebblous was selected as the lead organization of Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT 'Global Big Tech Development Program', receiving KRW 6 billion (~USD 4.3M) over 3 years — a public endorsement of its technological excellence and global growth potential. Building on this, Pebblous is now going beyond Data Clinic to develop the 'Data Greenhouse', an Agentic AI-based autonomous data operating system, and 'PebbloSim', a synthetic data generator for Physical AI. Through fully autonomous infrastructure spanning data diagnosis to generation, Pebblous aims to solve the critical bottleneck of data scarcity and quality in the global AI market.
